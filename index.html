Overview

The Challenge

  • Managing the full lifecycle of 2,000+ exhibitors across 100+ countries
  • Coordinating multiple internal teams without a centralised communication structure
  • Fixed live event deadline with zero flexibility
  • Frequent mid-project change requests from the client
  • Complex backend integrations across exhibitor data modules

My Approach

  • Coordinated full SDLC from requirements through to live deployment

  • Proactively resolved dependency conflicts via daily standups and dependency tracking

Case Study 04 · PICO / LTE China

Exhibitor Portal & Simplified Chinese Localisation

Overview

Client: PICO International · LTE 2025 & LTE 2026 · Learning & Teaching Expo, China · Exhibitor Portal + Exhibitor Listing + Full Simplified Chinese Localisation · 2024–2026 (ongoing).

The Challenge

  • Complete localisation into Simplified Chinese — UI, forms, emails, and listings
  • Significant language barrier between Indian dev team and Chinese client
  • Cultural differences in communication, approvals, and business etiquette
  • Multiple revision cycles for linguistic and cultural accuracy
  • Time zone differences compounding coordination complexity

My Approach

  • Structured communication protocol — all requirements documented in both languages
  • Dedicated localisation tracker — every element mapped to translation, review, approval
  • Buffer time built into roadmap for revision cycles — all completed within plan
  • Fixed weekly calls with bilingual written summaries after every session
