Case Study 04 · PICO / LTE China

Exhibitor Portal & Simplified Chinese Localisation

Overview

Client: PICO International · LTE 2025 & 2026 · China · Exhibitor Portal + Exhibitor Listing + Full Simplified Chinese Localisation · 2024–2026 (ongoing).

The Challenge

  • Complete localisation into Simplified Chinese — UI, forms, emails, listings
  • Language barrier between Indian dev team and Chinese client
  • Cultural differences in communication, approvals, business etiquette
  • Multiple revision cycles for linguistic and cultural accuracy
  • Time zone differences adding coordination complexity

My Approach

  • Structured communication protocol — requirements in both languages
  • Dedicated localisation tracker — every element mapped to translation, review, approval
  • Buffer time built into roadmap — all revision cycles within plan
  • Fixed weekly calls with bilingual written summaries

Results

✓Full Chinese localisation approved
✓100% on-time delivery
✓PICO retained for LTE 2026
✓Scope expanded for 2026
